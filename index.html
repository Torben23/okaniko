A culinary journey that celebrates vegetable delights and community empowerment.

At the heart of Atitlan Sunset Lodge lies the Okaniko restaurant, a culinary paradise dedicated to offering visitors a plant-based gastronomic adventure that transcends traditional taste boundaries. Set against the picturesque backdrop of Santa Cruz La Laguna, Okaniko is not just a culinary destination but a movement advocating for health, community, and environmental sustainability.

For those new to plant-based food, Okaniko invites them on a culinary discovery journey with a passionate and creative menu that offers a symphony of flavors, not only pleasing the taste buds but also promoting personal well-being. It's a celebration of vibrant, nutrient-rich ingredients that are not only exquisite in taste but also nourish both the body and mind.

The Okaniko restaurant is not just a place to eat; it is a testament to the transformative power of food and community. By choosing Okaniko, you will not only be savoring a delicious meal but also contributing to a positive domino effect that extends far beyond the dining table. Come and join us in redefining the possibilities of plant-based cuisine, one tasty bite at a time.

Why Us

Why Choose Our Restaurant

Health

Care

Desire to enrich the palates of both locals and foreigners by showcasing the incredible flavors that can be derived from plant-based ingredients without relying on meat or dairy. As well to allow you a different food experience.

Community

Empowerment

The restaurant proudly opens its doors to recent young Kak'chikel Mayan graduates from the cooking courses at the Training Center of Friends of Santa Cruz La Laguna (CECAP).

Environmental

Sustainability

By using locally sourced vegetables, the restaurant not only supports local farmers but also reduces its carbon footprint, contributing to a healthier planet. We also work hard to reduce our plastic production buying in bulks.

Eggplant Lasagna with Textured Tofu

Started: Roasted Tomato and Basil Soup

Explore a culinary journey with our innovative Textured Tofu-infused Eggplant Lasagna, a contemporary interpretation that draws inspiration from the luxurious heritage of Italian gastronomy. This innovative take pays homage to the conventional lasagna, replacing traditional meat with intricately textured tofu for a satisfying and flavorful twist. Accompanied by our Roasted Tomato and Basil Soup, this timeless combination captures the essence of Mediterranean flavors, a testament to our commitment to wholesome ingredients and inventive culinary artistry. Immerse yourself in the warmth of Italian tradition, reimagined for the contemporary palate, as we invite you to relish the seamless fusion of textures and tastes that define this extraordinary dining experience.

Thai Tofu Curry

Started: Lentil Soup

Indulge in the enticing world of flavors with our Thai Tofu Curry, where a harmonious symphony of vibrant tastes and aromatic spices awaits your palate. Paired as a prelude, our Lentil Soup captivates with its hearty warmth and wholesome goodness. The Thai Tofu Curry invites you to savor the delicate balance of creamy coconut milk, aromatic Thai herbs, and perfectly textured tofu. Each spoonful is a journey through the rich and diverse tastes of Thailand, expertly crafted to elevate your dining experience. Immerse yourself in the exotic allure of Thai cuisine, where bold flavors and comforting aromas harmonize to create an unforgettable dining sensation.

Vegan Mushroom Alfredo Pasta

Started: Creamy Broccoli Soup

Delve into the culinary realm with our Vegan Mushroom Alfredo Pasta, a contemporary reinterpretation of an Italian classic that adds a modern twist to the beloved favorite. Crafted to perfection, this plant-based delight features a velvety Alfredo sauce infused with earthy mushrooms, creating a harmonious blend that pays homage to traditional flavors while embracing the richness of vegan cuisine. As an appetizing prelude, indulge in our Creamy Broccoli Soup, a timeless comfort that warms the soul. Delight in the seamless fusion of flavors, where the hearty essence of mushrooms and the creamy Alfredo sauce dance on your palate. Join us in celebrating the heritage of Italian cuisine with a contemporary, vegan touch, as we invite you to savor every moment of this culinary journey.

Quinoa with Potatoes and Green Beans in Curry

Started: Vegetable Soup with Coconut Curry

Experience a culinary voyage with our Quinoa, Potatoes, and Green Beans in Curry, a tribute to the Andean region's rich agricultural history. Celebrating the versatility of quinoa, a sacred grain from South America, this dish is a fusion of ancient grains and vibrant curry flavors. Begin your journey with the Vegetable Soup infused with Coconut Curry, offering a global twist on comfort with a medley of seasonal vegetables. Our creation tells a story of culinary exploration, connecting Andean traditions with the aromatic allure of coconut-infused curries, providing a savory experience that transcends time and taste.
